Our Story

How Black Oak Got Started

I started BOPC as a sole proprietor in 2017, as a response to the poor craftsmanship and dishonesty I had seen in the construction industry, and more specifically, in the restoration and remodel world. Black Oak Property Care LLC was founded on the principles of honesty, integrity and making sure things are done the right way, the first time, and without the inflated costs we see more of daily. I take pride in my craft and my creations, and I hold all my employees and sub-contractors to the same standard, let's continue making our community one to be proud of!
